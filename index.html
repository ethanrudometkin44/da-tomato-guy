What makes Da Tomato Guy special?

Grown and made right here in Hawaiʻi, Da Tomato Guy is all about fresh ingredients, big flavor, and a whole lot of aloha. Every jar is small-batch and handcrafted — because good salsa takes time and heart.

The Flavor That Sets Us Apart

Simple, real ingredients — no fillers, no shortcuts. Just the good stuff that makes every scoop taste like home.

Homegrown Tomatoes

Grown right in our own garden. Sun-ripened and picked fresh for every batch — the heart of every jar.

Fresh Peppers

Mild or spicy, our peppers are fresh and local. They bring the heat and the flavor — never from a can.

Fresh Cilantro

Hand-chopped and added fresh. That bright, herby finish that makes our salsa taste alive.

White Onion

Finely diced for the perfect crunch and bite. Balances the sweetness of the tomatoes in every scoop.
